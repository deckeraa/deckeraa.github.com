Infinite Sequences and Series Cheat Sheet

  •  Connecting to the UWPlatt network
  •  

Throughout my time tutoring Calculus II, I have found that oftentimes people have a hard time remembering all the series tests, so I put together this cheat sheet last year.

It contains all of the series tests, not including power series, that are covered in Chapter 12 of Stewart Calculus, 6th ed. Additionally, important definitions are included.

Most people will want the pdf: series.pdf but the source is also availble for modification: series.tex
